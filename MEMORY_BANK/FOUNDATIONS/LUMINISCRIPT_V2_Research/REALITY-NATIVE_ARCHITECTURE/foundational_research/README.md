**Light-Based Reality-Native Processing**

Investigates optical approaches where computation happens at light speed:

- Coherent interference as logic gates

- Photonic neural networks with ultra-low latency

**Key Insight**: Light naturally performs Fourier transforms and wavelength-parallel processing, making optical systems ideal for spectral computation.

**Mathematics Without Discretization**
